Assistant Professor, Department of Integrated Information Technology

Aoyama Gakuin University

About

Naoshi Kaneko (金子 直史) is an assistant professor in Department of Integrated Information Technology, Aoyama Gakuin University. His research interests include computer vision, pattern recognition, non-verbal behavior generation and machine learning.
